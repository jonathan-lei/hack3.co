What is Hack3?

Hack3 is a fun, 24 hour hackathon made for high school students, both new and experienced. During this time you will develop and present a project for cool prizes. Most importantly, you will learn new and useful skills that will help you on your computer science journey.

Our Goal

In the palm of your hands, you hold a computer a thousand times faster than the Apollo Guidance Computer. Yet, this power remains mostly untapped. Hack3 aims to change that. We want you to use technology for good, preserving privacy and ethics.

We need to nurture a new generation of determined programmers and give them the ability to make change for good. Our aim is to provide an opportunity for aspiring leaders of the tech world to bring their own ideas to fruition and create a better world for future generations.
